About GreenEdge

Transforming Outdoor Spaces Since 2010

At GreenEdge, we turn your outdoor dreams into reality. From home gardens to commercial landscapes, we bring creativity, precision, and passion into every project. Our goal is to build sustainable green spaces that inspire life and relaxation.

With a dedicated team of designers, horticulturists, and maintenance experts, we’ve been serving communities with care and excellence for over two decades.

Explore Our Services

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, high-quality property maintenance services — from landscaping and cleaning to security and irrigation — through a dedicated team, innovative solutions, and a commitment to exceeding client expectations.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of comprehensive property care, transforming outdoor and indoor spaces into beautiful, safe, and well-maintained environments for every client.

Our Journey

How We Grew Through The Years

2010 – The Beginning

GreenEdge was established with a mission to provide reliable property maintenance services to residential and commercial clients.

2015 – Expansion

Grew our dedicated team to 15 skilled professionals to handle an increasing number of clients.

2018 – Services Diversified

Added irrigation, drainage, painting, and urban gardening to our portfolio, becoming a one-stop property solutions provider.

2020 – Security Solutions Introduced

Expanded services to include electric fencing and CCTV installations for comprehensive property protection..

2021 – Over 1,000 Happy Clients

Reached a milestone of 1,000+ satisfied clients.

2025 – Today

GreenEdge continues to provide exceptional property maintenance services, blending expertise, reliability, and innovative solutions to keep every property in top condition.
